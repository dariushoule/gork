# Game loop
- big picture: create a thread that represents the current campaign, only one runs at a time
- campaigns are just players typing creative messages to the gamemaster and the gamemaster deciding outcomes 
- when all monsters defeated the gamemaster makes a new random campaign in the gork universe (use player behavior and description from previous 4 games to evolve a persistent and growing narrative on planet gork)

---

- Check if gamestate.db exists (sqlite), if it doesn't create it and prepare to store
    - the description and title for the current campaign 
    - players
        - players are created when they interact with the game the first time
        - tied to the discord account sending the message obv
        - players have inventories which hold arbitrary items
- A campaign runs as long as monsters and bosses are still present
    - At the start of a campaign generate all new content and create a thread in the channel for it, all interaction is scoped to this thread
    - campaigns consist of between 8-13 normal monsters, 3 minibossses (randomly placed), and one major boss at the end
        - All monsters have 100 hp, but actions taken by players reduce this less when the monster is harder, rolls are harder too
    - The names of the monsters are random in the format of name (appropriate to type, more elaborate when boss or miniboss), title, and monster type.
        - E.G. Gormuck the Strange Ork
        - E.G. Tinkle Spork the Effeminate Space Ranger
    - When monsters die they drop either mundane items, special items, or exotic items. special and exotic drop more proportionally to difficulty
        - Loot drops are completely generated by the game master (this game is silly to they're totally random crap, MORE THAN typical RPG stuff)
            - mundane example: Desk Lamp of Illumination
            - mundane example: Stolen Traffic Light of Crime
            - special example: Magic Odourous Tube Sock of Withering Disgust
            - exotic item: Crushing Lazer Grenade of Galahads Holy Ballsack
            - exotic item: One day Blinding Stew of Powerfull Piss Shivers
        - Everyone who participates in the thread gets loot chance when a monster dies (randomly assigned)
        - normal monsters: mundane loot 1-3 items,
        - minibossses: mundane loot 1-3 items, special items 1-3
        - bossess: mundane loot 1-6 items, special items 1-6, exotic items 1-6
- Turns:
    - When a player types a message in the thread it is their action, the gamemaster resolve it in order
        - If the monster the player intended is dead just interpret it where possible as the next one or come up with a fun way to describe the miss (no-op). up to the gamemaster
        - Players have 100hp, recover 5hp per round base unless they use an item that can heal (up to the gamemaster to decide how much something heals based on logic)
        - Actions are just things that lower monster HP or increase player HP (theirs or others) (players will send arbitrary text for the gamemaster to interpret), the gamemaster maintains HP transparently behind the scenes. Don't restrict to combat, players creativity is key.
            - If users actions truly cannot be reply to them and say they need to clarify what they're doing. 
            - IF the users goals are lofty make them roll a difficult random number 0-100, hp damage is proportionally to risk
            - IF the users goals are easy they roll and easier random number 0-100, but lower HP damage.
            - Users can ask to use items as part of their action, if they do the item is consumed (1-time use always)
                - Make sure the user knows how it was consumed
                - Items can increase damage, make for better odds, or just add comedy - thats up to the gamemaster to decide how to incorporate. 
        - When the turn is resolved you respond to the player and tell them the outcome of what happened (let the random number 0-100 guide the success and tell a compelling DM story about how it shook out)
            - If the monster dies describe it to everyone and put a summary of everyones loot.  
        - Players can have status effects (arbitrary, just text in the DB that gets resolved each time they take a turn by the gamemaster)
        - Players can be incapacitated (bad rolls on actions can land them in space jail, break their leg so they need to recover, put them in a prism of immobility, etc... examples)
            - Incapacitation lasts a real world amount of 0-300 seconds. The worse the turn that landed them incapacitated is the longer they need (weighted random). 
            - Time can be reduced by other players helping, same kind of difficulty rules / item interaction as before
    - All actions are in the context of defeating the monster 
    - Keep all things that have happened in this campaign in some kind of thread and remember them so that there's story continuity between turns (can we like prompt cache or something?)

If a user types a command (!something) delete the message and send:
- !me
    - them their current inventory,status effects, if they're incapacitated and why and HP
- !party
    - all players HP, status effects, incapacitation
